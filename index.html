To offer a summary of myself, my name is Paul Walker, an IT enthusist located in South West Scotland. I've spend much of my life surrounded by computers and technology which therefore encourged me to puruse a career in the IT industry. To aid in this, I attended Dumfries & Galloway College and studied for a Higher National Diplomia in IT to build upon experience I had learned during my own time in technical support aspects. I then attended University of South West Scotland in Dumfries to study an Honours Degree in Computing Science graduating in late 2019. Both my areas of education have given me insights in various areas of IT and I'm excited to gain work experience to decide which aspect is the best fit for me.

To gain some more experience in technical support, I volunteered with Lead Scotland to act as Digital Skills Mentor which was aimed at supporting vulnerable individuals to help setup various devices such as tablets and laptops to improve their confidence in a growing digital world. I also volunteered at Apex Scotland to gain work experience in web development before finally gaining employment with them. During my time with Apex Scotland I built on my web development skills while being introduced to the world of Salesforce and have since been studying to gain an administration credential.

Beyond this I'm still interested in various aspects of IT and currently looking for opportunities to broaden my experience.
